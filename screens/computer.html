Contents of IT

What will I learn?

  • Being Organized
  • Working with others
  • Researching a topic
  • Developing a Computer program
  • Developing a digital product
  • Creating a digital animated graph
  • Developing a personal progression plan
  • Solving a technical IT problems

What skills will I obtain?

  • Produce a CV and personal action plan
  • Gain valuable team working and communication skills by taking part in the events
  • Learn about more local businesses and how they operate
  • Develop your confidence in presentation

How will I be assessed?

  • This is an assignment course so there are no exams.
  • However, You will be continually assessed throughout the programme through assigments, practical activities and observations carried out by the teachers.
